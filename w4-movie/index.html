ABOUT the show Friends

Friends is a show that follows the lives of six main characters in New York: Rachel Green, Phoebe Buffay, Monica Geller, Ross Geller, Joey Tribbiani, and Chandler Bing. Throughout the show, the characters learn how to navigate all the drama of their mid 20s to early 30s and what it means to be friends.

Creators: David Crane and Marta Kauffman
