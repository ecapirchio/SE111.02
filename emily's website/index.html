Biography

Welcome to my homepage. Emily Sullivan is a freshman at the University of Vermont.

Emily is studying animal science with a minor in medical diagnostics. Emily is from Cranston, Rhode Island, where she loves to go to the beach and spend time with her pet cats, Louie and Luca. She hopes to one day be a veterinarian, but if that doesn't work out, she will do research in animal health.

At UVM, she is part of the Dairy Club and Pre-Veterinary club, where she gets to spend time with the dairy cows on campus. She is also apart of the ski and snowboard club, where she goes skiing at Smuggler's Notch on the weekends. She has some experience with animals; she has shadowed a veterinarian at Warwick Animal Hospital in Warwick Rhode Island, she volunteered at a cat shelter.

During school break, she is a starbucks barista and loves to create new drinks and connect with her regular customers. In her free time, she loves to spend time with her cats, go to local restaurants with friends, and crochet clothes and stuffed animals.
